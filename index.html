From the ashes we rise

Rugged Nation is a project that will give Rugged NFT projects a new home. We are giving back a self governing community where ideas and voting decide on the future road map and utilities of Rugged Nation.

Roadmap

  • Project logo
  • Web landing page (Wallet connection, blog, web design, etc...)
  • Dev work on vault
  • Dev work on mint process
  • Utility (Token, pfp generator, community based, more to come...
  • Staking (in development)
  • Project intro video (in development)
  • Email
  • Twitter page
  • Instagram page
  • Discord setp-up
  • Growing our community
  • Morph a Ruuged Project into a Rugged Nation NFT
  • Community votes for Top collections to be included
  • Top 5/10 collections will be selected
  • Users will be bring NFT from selected rug collection and lock it within our vault
  • Our vault then produces a replacement nft
